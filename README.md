# Langton's Ant

A simulation of the Langton's Ant algorithm. An "ant" will spawn in the center of the grid and move according to the following rules:

> If on a black square, turn 90 degrees clockwise;
> If on a white square, turn 90 degrees anticlockwise;
> Flips the color of the square it's on and move 1 square forward.

You can press P to pause and R to reset the simulation, as shown in the bottom left text description.
